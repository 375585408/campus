Michael J McGuire简介：
Dr. McGuire received his BS degree from University of Pennsylvania and MS and PHD degrees from Drexel University.   Currently a member of National Academy of Engineering, he was Assistant General Manager of The Metropolitan Water District of Southern California, President and Founder of McGuire Environmental Consultants, Inc., and Vice President of Malcolm Pirnie, Inc.  He served as Vice President of American Water Works Association, Member of Board of Directors of Association of Metropolitan Water Agencies, and a member of various Technologies Working Groups for US Environmental Protection Agency.  He has received numerous honors and awards, including AP Black Research Award, George Warren Fuller Award, and Election to National Academy of Engineering.  He is specialized in drinking water quality and treatment processes, especially in trace organic contaminant control, water disinfection and disinfection byproduct control, and taste & odor control.  Recently, he published a book, The Chlorine Revolution: Water Disinfection and the Fight to Save Lives.

解跃峰简介：
   解跃峰教授毕业于清华大学，取得学士、硕士和博士学位， 并在麻省州立大学从事博士后研究工作。现任宾州州立大学首府学院环境工程教授， 创建了小型公共供水系统技术服务中心和环境培训中心并担任中心主任。 曾担任美国水协有机污染物控制委员会主任委员,美国化学学会宾州东南部分会主席，和北美华人环境工程与科学教授学会首任主席。是国际水协会士（Fellow） 和2010年加拿大富布莱特讲席研究员。有专著“Disinfection Byproducts in Drinking Water: Formation, Analysis, and Control” 和编著“Disinfection By-Products in Drinking Water: Occurence, Formation, Health Effects and Control”。于2010年入选中组部“千人计划”，在清华大学环境学院开展教学与科研工作。 同时担任住房和城乡建设部水专项咨询专家委员会委员。
